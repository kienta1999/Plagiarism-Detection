Fans of “This Is Us” clearly love the show but, apparently, some are not very good listeners.

In a nod to the NBC drama’s much-anticipated season finale Tuesday night, “The Tonight Show” writer Arthur Meyer interviewed the show’s followers about it ― but badly mispronounced the title.

He called “This Is Us” everything from “Miss the Bus” to “Whip His Nuts” and the fans in Rockefeller Center didn’t seem to notice.
