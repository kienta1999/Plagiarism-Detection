The newest “Avengers: Infinity War” trailer dropped last week, and it’s hard to imagine how it could be any better, short of the full movie.

But now we know how. Just add Deadpool.

Istanbul-based digital animator Saruhan Saral replaced just about every character in the official “Infinity War” trailer with Deadpool, and even got a voice actor who sounds strikingly similar to Deadpool actor Ryan Reynolds.
