This cave house is located in Ortahisar, one of the most authentic villages in the famous Cappadocia region . It has contained private deluxe suite and three additional double cave rooms. It can be rented entirely, or as individual separate rooms. It has outdoor terraces that are perfect for viewing the village with the ancient rock-cut Ortahisar Castle. It's located two minutes up to the village center where you’ll find shops, restaurants, cafes, ATMs and transport to all the major tourist sites.
